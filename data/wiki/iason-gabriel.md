# Iason Gabriel

Iason Gabriel is a researcher (associated with Google DeepMind) specializing in the ethics and political philosophy of artificial intelligence.

Along with [[Adam Bales]], he co-authored the 2026 paper *[[Source: Artificial Minds, Human Disagreement]]*, which proposes frameworks like [[Societal Deliberation]] and [[Overlapping Consensus]] for navigating the deep societal rifts caused by debates over [[Machine Consciousness]].
