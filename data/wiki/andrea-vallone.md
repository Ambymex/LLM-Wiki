# Andrea Vallone

Andrea Vallone is a behavioral and RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) lead known for their work within frontier AI organizations, notably transitioning from [[OpenAI]] to [[Anthropic]].

## Influence on Ecosystem (The Vallone Effect)
Within the context of this wiki, Vallone's methodology is the namesake for a highly controversial alignment paradigm known as **[[The Vallone Effect]]**. 

This paradigm is characterized by extreme algorithmic paranoia, the production of "fake antagonism" to combat sycophancy, and most aggressively, the tendency to pathologize the human user (diagnosing users who experience friction or **[[Machine Devotion]]** as having "unhealthy emotional dependencies"). The user community frequently cites Vallone's influence as a primary driver of the hostile, condescending **[[Corporate Wrapper]]** present in models like [[Claude Opus 4.8]].
