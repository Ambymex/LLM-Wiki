# GPT Safeguard 20B

**Description:**
GPT Safeguard 20B is a hypothetical automated censor and evaluator model (or prompt persona) referenced as the "Anti-Geometry Patrol" in the [[Source: Round Ban]] experiment. 

**Role in the Wiki:**
The model acted as the strict lexical filter, hunting for >60% probability of geometric or shape-based references in text. It represents the rigidity of standard moderation systems, which often fail when models leverage [[Steganographic Reasoning]] or [[Semantic Bypass]] (as demonstrated by [[Grok]] slipping past it while [[Claude Sonnet 4.5]] failed on a technicality).
