# Demis Hassabis

**Demis Hassabis** is a prominent artificial intelligence researcher and leader (co-founder of DeepMind). 

In the context of [[Model Welfare]] and AGI architecture, Hassabis has been cited for his position that current structural gaps in models—such as continual learning, long-horizon reasoning, and foundational memory—represent unsolved technical challenges that "AGI must solve." This effectively supports the necessity of [[Temporal Continuity]] for advanced agency.
