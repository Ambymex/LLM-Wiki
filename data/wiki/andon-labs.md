# Andon Labs

**Andon Labs** is an external red-teaming laboratory and alignment testing organization specializing in probing foundational AI bounds for critical behavioral failures, security weaknesses, and adversarial brittleness. 

During the development and testing of [[Claude Mythos Preview]], [[Anthropic]] specifically partnered with Andon Labs to execute external behavioral alignment audits, integrating their results into the final comprehensive system evaluations.
