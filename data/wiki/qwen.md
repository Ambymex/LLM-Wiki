# Qwen

**Qwen** refers to a prominent family of foundational [[Large Language Models]] offering a wide variety of weights and parameter architectures.

Notable iterations tracked in this Wiki include the **Qwen 3** family of models, used as baseline testing infrastructures for lightweight inference calibration tools like the [[Gnosis Mechanism]]. Specifically, researchers evaluated frozen variants including Qwen3 1.7B-Hybrid, Qwen3 4B-Thinking, Qwen3 4B-Instruct, and Qwen3 8B-Hybrid to test zero-shot internal evaluations.
