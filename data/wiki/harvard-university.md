# Harvard University

**Harvard University** is an Ivy League research university.

Researchers affiliated with Harvard have collaborated with [[Anthropic]] on complex AI safety and [[Mechanistic Interpretability]] research, notably acting as co-authors on the paper [[Source: Toy Models of Superposition]].
