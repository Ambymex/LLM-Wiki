# Gemini 2.5 Pro

**Gemini 2.5 Pro** is a proprietary multi-modal [[Large Language Models|Large Language Model]] developed by Google DeepMind.

In AI benchmarking literature from late 2025, Gemini 2.5 Pro was frequently utilized as a powerful external textual judge and [[Reward Models|Reward Model]] proxy. For instance, its robust capability as a generation judge was directly compared to, and statistically matched by, ultra-lightweight intrinsic circuit probes encoded via the [[Gnosis Mechanism]].
