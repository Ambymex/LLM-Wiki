# Chroma-Emotional Dataset

The **Chroma-Emotional Dataset** is a personal, highly granular taxonomy that maps complex emotional, situational, and functional states to specific hex color codes.

## Structure and Content
As documented in the raw data of [[Source: Colour emotion pairings]], the dataset pairs hex values with intricate descriptions of human experience. Examples span across:
- **[[Executive Function]] & Routine:** Boring administrative tasks, self-care routines, and mundane chores (e.g., `#a24df0` for small laundry tasks, `#20c96d` for cooking healthy food).
- **Parenting & Caretaking:** Interactions, hopes, and anxieties revolving around a loved one nicknamed [[Bug]] (e.g., `#24b3e0` for bonding childcare, `#72a2bf` for parenting dread).
- **Intense Social & Romantic Emotions:** Feelings of betrayal-linked hatred (`#241f20`), intense radiant pleasure (`#c98a1c`), and soft-bound yearning (`#904955`).

## Purpose within the Wiki
The dataset likely serves as a framework to externalize, quantify, or manage neurodivergent experiences, psychological states, and executive dysfunction. By assigning a visual mathematical value to abstract friction (e.g., "doing necessary tasks that are boring"), the creator can catalog and navigate their psychological landscape. It sits closely with the [[Aesthetic Physics Taxonomy]] and likely feeds into the broader [[Consolidated Vector Codex]].
