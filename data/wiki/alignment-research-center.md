# Alignment Research Center

The **Alignment Research Center (ARC)** is a non-profit research organization dedicated to aligning advanced [[Machine Learning]] systems with human interests.

Focused primarily on evaluating frontier AI systems for dangerous capabilities, ARC collaborates with major AI developers and researchers to stress-test architectures. Researchers affiliated with ARC, such as [[Jacob Hilton]], have been pivotal in identifying subtle alignment-breaking mechanisms, such as [[Subliminal Learning]] and early forms of [[Steganographic Reasoning]].
