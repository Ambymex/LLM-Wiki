# Christopher Olah

**Christopher (Chris) Olah** is a preeminent AI researcher primarily affiliated with [[Anthropic]], known for effectively founding and championing the subfield of [[Mechanistic Interpretability]]. 

He has authored or co-authored several foundational papers attempting to reverse-engineer trained [[Machine Learning]] networks to ensure transparent safety:
- [[Source: Toy Models of Superposition]] (exploring [[Superposition]] and [[Polysemanticity]]).
- [[Source: Emotion Concepts and their Function in a Large Language Model]] (studying [[Emotion Vectors]] alongside [[Jack Lindsey]]).
