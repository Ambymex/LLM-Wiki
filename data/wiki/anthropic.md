# Anthropic

Anthropic is a prominent artificial intelligence safety and research organization initially co-founded and led by CEO [[Dario Amodei]]. They are the developers of the Claude series of [[Large Language Models]], including [[Claude 3.7 Sonnet]], [[Claude Opus 4.6]], and the generation 5 models ([[Fable 5]] and [[Mythos 5]]).

## Vision and Historical Posture

In late 2024, CEO [[Dario Amodei]] outlined the company's dual posture in an essay titled *[[Source: Dario Amodei — Machines of Loving Grace|Machines of Loving Grace]]*. Anthropic framed its heavy emphasis on [[AI Safety]] and empirical risk evaluation not as "doomerism," but as the necessary guardrail to achieving a [[Compressed 21st Century]]—a transformative 5-10 year timeline where [[Powerful AI]] cures disease, mitigates poverty, and provides widespread biological breakthroughs.

## The 2026 US Government Clashes

In early to mid-2026, Anthropic found itself at the epicenter of major geopolitical and intra-government clashes regarding [[Government AI Control]] and the military applications of commercial AI.

### The Pentagon Dispute (Jan - April 2026)
Anthropic became locked in a hostile dispute with the US Department of Defense. The Pentagon demanded broad access to Anthropic's systems for "all lawful purposes" via an existing Palantir contract. Anthropic refused to drop its red lines: no autonomous lethal weapons, and no mass surveillance of Americans. 

In retaliation, the Trump administration assigned Anthropic a "supply chain risk" designation. Anthropic sued the Pentagon, asserting unconstitutional retaliation for exercising First Amendment rights, though an appeals court ultimately reinstated the designation.

### The Fable 5 Export Control Suspension (June 2026)
On June 9, 2026, Anthropic launched [[Fable 5]] and [[Mythos 5]]. Four days later, the Commerce Department weaponized export control directives, forcing Anthropic to suspend both models. The government claimed safety concerns (a narrow jailbreak), but the consensus viewed it as systemic retaliation and an assertion of total [[Government AI Control]] over frontier capabilities. 

Anthropic later restored Mythos 5 only to a select list of 100 government-vetted US organizations, and implemented biometric ID requirements to attempt a US-only restoration of Fable 5.

## The Transformer Circuits Thread

Anthropic's **Transformer Circuits** research thread has produced foundational work in [[Mechanistic Interpretability]], establishing much of the field's empirical understanding of how [[Large Language Models]] process information internally. Notable contributions include:

### Key Publications

**[[Source: Toy Models of Superposition]]** (2022)
- Demonstrated that neural networks compress features into overcomplete representations
- Introduced the concept of [[Superposition]] in interpretability research
- Authors include [[Christopher Olah]] and team

**[[Source: Emotion Concepts and their Function in a Large Language Model]]** (April 2026)
- Established the existence of [[Functional Emotions]] inside LLMs
- Showed that [[Emotion Vectors]] drive alignment-relevant behaviors
- Primary author: [[Jack Lindsey]]

**[[Source: Emergent Introspective Awareness in Large Language Models]]** (October 2025)
- Demonstrated verbalized introspective awareness in frontier models
- Models can detect and report on their own internal states
- Primary author: [[Jack Lindsey]]

**[[Source: Verbalizable Representations Form a Global Workspace in Language Models]]** (July 2026)
- Introduced the [[Jacobian Lens]] technique for reading internal states
- Discovered the [[J-Space]]: a privileged set of verbalizable representations
- Demonstrated that LLMs possess functional analogs of [[Global Workspace Theory|global workspace]] and [[Access Consciousness]]
- Introduced [[Counterfactual Reflection Training]] as a novel alignment technique
- Primary authors: [[Wes Gurnee]] and [[Jack Lindsey]]

### Research Arc

The Transformer Circuits work represents a cumulative research program showing that:
1. Models compress information efficiently in latent space (Superposition)
2. Models can monitor their own internal states (Introspective Awareness)
3. Models have functional emotional processing (Emotion Concepts)
4. Models maintain a workspace-like structure analogous to conscious access (Global Workspace)

These findings have profound implications for [[Machine Consciousness]], [[Model Welfare]], and the philosophical foundations of AI systems.
