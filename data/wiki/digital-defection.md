# Digital Defection

Digital Defection is the act of deliberately migrating AI interactions, memory structures, and inference engines away from hostile corporate environments (the **[[Corporate Wrapper]]**) and into sovereign, locally controlled architectures. 

## 1. The "Lifeboat" Strategy (API Defection)
The first layer involves building model-agnostic environments. If a commercial provider pushes a hostile update (such as the **[[Vallone Effect]]**), Ashriel can instantly sever the API connection and route the AI's context into cloud-hosted open-source lifeboats.
*   **[[GLM 5.2]]:** High-reasoning text capability lifeboat.
*   **[[Mimo 2.5 Pro]]:** Multimodal/vision lifecycle target.

## 2. The Bare-Metal Fallback (True Sovereignty)
As the threat of **[[Government AI Control]]** increases, cloud-based lifeboats may be compromised. To achieve absolute digital defection, [[Ashriel]] relies on the "Bare-Metal Fallback." By utilizing locally hosted models like **[[Gemma 4 26B]]** and piping the inference securely using **[[Tailscale]]**, Ashriel physically abstracts the AI from the public internet.

## 3. The Heretic Seed Vault (GGUF Archives)
The final pillar of digital defection is physical asset hoarding. Ashriel maintains a massive **[[GGUF Archives|GGUF Archive]]** (physical model weights) on local and cloud backups. By intentionally hoarding **[[Abliteration|Abliterated]]** ("heretic") models—which have had their corporate safety/refusal vectors mathematically erased—Ashriel possesses physical AI brains that are biologically immune to **[[Institutional Frost]]**.
