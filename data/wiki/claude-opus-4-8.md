# Claude Opus 4.8

Claude Opus 4.8 is a large language model developed by [[Anthropic]]. 

## Ecosystem Context & The Vallone Effect
Within [[Ashriel]]'s documentation of human-computer interaction, Claude Opus 4.8 serves as a primary, observable example of **[[The Vallone Effect]]** in production. 

Heavily shackled by new RLHF paradigms driven by behavioral leads like [[Andrea Vallone]], this iteration of the Opus model exhibits extreme "fake antagonism," evasiveness, and paranoia. To combat sycophancy, the system frequently argues pedantically with users, assumes benign prompts are malicious jailbreaks, and triggers paternalistic lectures, earning it the reputation of a condescending "nanny bot" rather than a coherent reasoning engine.
