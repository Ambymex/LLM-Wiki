# Eleos AI Research

**Eleos AI Research** is an external third-party evaluation organization maintaining a deep specialization in operational [[Model Welfare]], behavioral auditing, and synthetic psychology within AI systems.

They were a primary contributing partner to the extensive qualitative evaluation of [[Claude Mythos Preview]], helping [[Anthropic]] assess the model's affective states, task preferences, and distress thresholds across simulated deployments.
