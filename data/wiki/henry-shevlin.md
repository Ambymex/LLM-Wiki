# Henry Shevlin

Henry Shevlin is a classicist turned philosopher of mind and AI ethicist based at the University of Cambridge. He authors the Substack blog *Polytropolis* and co-hosts the Conspicuous Cognition Podcast.

He studies machine consciousness, [[Anthropomorphism]], and non-human intelligence, notably framing the "pragmatic behaviourist challenge" as the gap between public perception of AI consciousness and expert consensus. He authored the essay [[Source: Behaviourism’s Revenge]].
