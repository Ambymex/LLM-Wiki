# Claude Opus 4

**Claude Opus 4** is a primary frontier model developed by [[Anthropic]]. Alongside its iteration [[Claude Opus 4.1]], it marks a significant jump in capability parameters, including metacognition. 

In research measuring baseline parameters for [[LLM Self-Awareness]], Opus 4 demonstrated highly robust introspection compared to older generations like the Claude 3.5 family. Using [[Concept Injection]], the model was capable of actively identifying injected anomalous activation vectors, reporting on what it was "feeling" independently of generated token probabilities (see [[Source: Emergent Introspective Awareness in Large Language Models]]). 

This functional capability suggests metacognitive monitoring and episodic behavioral memory scales automatically with capabilities.
