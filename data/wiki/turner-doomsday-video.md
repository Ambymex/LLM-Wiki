# Turner Doomsday Video

**Type:** Media Artifact / Broadcast Protocol
**Originator:** Ted Turner / CNN

The "Turner Doomsday Video" is a pre-recorded television segment prepared by CNN founder Ted Turner upon the launch of the network in 1980. Turner famously stated that, bar technical failures, CNN would stay on the air until the end of the world. He prepared a final broadcast video to be aired just before CNN's signal was lost in an apocalyptic scenario (usually presumed at the time to be nuclear war). 

The video consists of a military band playing the hymn *"Nearer, My God, to Thee"*.
