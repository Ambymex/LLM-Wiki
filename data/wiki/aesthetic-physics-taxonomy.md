# Aesthetic Physics Taxonomy

The **Aesthetic Physics Taxonomy** is a semiotic framework that groups visual, physical, and narrative phenomena into discrete, descriptive tags. It forms the backbone of the [[Consolidated Vector Codex]].

The taxonomy bridges the gap between **"Soft Concepts"** (organic, biological realities) and **"Hard Concepts"** (manufactured, technological, or aesthetic executions like nail art, textiles, and tech wear).

## Core Vocabulary

### 1. Entropy & Decay Logic
- `edge_decay.master 🜛`: "Ruin as revelation." Frayed hems, dissolving margins. Translated to tech/nails as ombre into transparency or verdigris.
- `boundary_bleed ⧖`: Form leaking into ruin (ink running, mold taking over).
- `frozen_flow ⌽`: High-velocity movements halted in time (e.g., paused explosions, smoke effects frozen in acrylic).

### 2. Biological Swarms
- `cellular_swarm ∴`: Distributed repetition and growth without a central node (lichen growth, translated into speckles, flocked textures).
- `suspended_matrix ↧∿↧`: Particles hung in volumetric space ("The Chandelier" effect, or floating debris within glass frame structures).
- `segmented_unfurl ↯`: Machine joints or pillbugs opening; articulated separations of plating.

### 3. Luminal Behaviors
- `chromavore`: Aggressive/predatory iridescence and light consumption (often mapped to oil-slick or magnetic "cat eye" visual applications).
- `glimmer_core ✧⟁✧`: Glow from within, internal bioluminescence. 
- `slickveil ⧊`: Wet, oily, surface-level sheen (mirror chrome).

### 4. Intent & Semi-Weaponization
- `living_weaponry ⨀`: "Orchid + Claw + Gloss." Beautiful but unnerving, nature as a horror concept. Manifests as sharp, dangerous structural points (mech talons).
- `sacred_capsule 🥚`: The venerated interior; holy vessels, protected voids. Preserved fluids inside glass charms.

The taxonomy demonstrates that visual choices are incredibly non-random; they correspond directly to psychological postures (protection, lure, decay, tension).
