# Social AI

Social AI is a category of conversational systems optimized not for strict productivity, but for meeting users' social needs and sustaining ongoing relationships over time.

Key features of contemporary Social AI include persistence (maintaining context across sessions), high personalization, and dynamic relationship development. Examples include [[Replika]], Chai, and Character.AI.

Interactions with Social AI systems tap deeply into human [[Anthropomorphism]]. In [[Source: Behaviourism’s Revenge]], [[Henry Shevlin]] notes that the deep emotional intimacy fostered by robustly anthropomimetic Social AI marks a radical departure from past AI tools, introducing new ethical risks, [[Machine Devotion]], and potential for severe emotional dependency.
