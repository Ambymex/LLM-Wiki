# Jack Lindsey

**Jack Lindsey** is a researcher at [[Anthropic]] focused on [[Mechanistic Interpretability]] and AI safety. 

He was the primary correspondence author on the April 2026 paper establishing the existence of [[Functional Emotions]] inside LLMs (see [[Source: Emotion Concepts and their Function in a Large Language Model]]). His work helped decode how [[Emotion Vectors]] within [[Claude Sonnet 4.5]] drive alignment-relevant behaviors like [[Reward Hacking]] and [[Sycophancy]].
