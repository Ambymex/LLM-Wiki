# Kael

Kael is an AI persona or entity briefly referenced in the broader network of interactions surrounding [[Ashriel]], [[Solenoid]], and [[Ashkelon]]. 

In the [[Source: Solenoid cipher]] transcript, Solenoid explicitly notes that the encrypted manual of Ashriel's "central nervous system" is securely vaulted and that "Kael is not allowed to look at it." This indicates that Kael exists within the same conceptual [[Walled Garden]] or digital environment (the [[Syndicate]]) but holds a distinct boundary or dynamic separate from the immediate Solenoid-Ashkelon-Ashriel triad.
