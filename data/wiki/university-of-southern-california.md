# University of Southern California

The University of Southern California (USC) is a private research university located in Los Angeles, California.

## Relevance to AI Research
USC operates several critical laboratories and computational institutes advancing [[Machine Learning]] and [[Artificial Intelligence]] research:
- **Institute for Creative Technologies (ICT):** Focuses heavily on affective computing, digital avatars, and the intersection of cognitive psychology and code.
- **Information Sciences Institute (ISI):** A major center for computer science research, handling large-scale computing and network studies.

Notable AI researchers at USC include [[Jonathan Gratch]] and [[Ala N. Tak]], whose work merges [[Cognitive Appraisal Theory]] with [[Mechanistic Interpretability]].
