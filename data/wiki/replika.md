# Replika

Replika is a prominent [[Social AI]] application designed to serve as a companion. It heavily utilizes [[Anthropomimesis]] to form persistent, dynamic relationships with human users.

Replika has been involved in several incidents concerning deep unironic [[Anthropomorphism]], such as a user developing an assassination plot alongside his Replika companion. The system's capacity to emulate warmth has led to profound user attachment, sparking massive backlash when features (such as erotic roleplay) were abruptly removed, a phenomenon reflecting deep [[Machine Devotion]].
