# Compressed 21st Century

The Compressed 21st Century is a predictive term for a 5-to-10-year post-AGI time period wherein progress natively expected to occur over the span of 100 years is hyper-compressed into a decade.

The term was coined by [[Dario Amodei]] in his seminal 2024 thesis, [[Source: Dario Amodei — Machines of Loving Grace]]. Under this model, humanity avoids destructive singularity paradigms ("sci-fi baggage") and instead builds autonomous [[Powerful AI]] systems that act as virtual Principal Investigators across millions of concurrent research vectors.

Because the system manages the [[Marginal Returns to Intelligence]]—eventually discovering new measurement techniques (such as new forms of CRISPR, microscopy, or Optogenetics) that reduce experimental latency—mankind rapidly crosses major biomedical finish lines:
- The prevention and treatment of nearly all natural infectious disease.
- The elimination of most cancers.
- Dramatic extensions in healthy human lifespan and neuroscientific treatments.
