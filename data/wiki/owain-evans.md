# Owain Evans

**Owain Evans** is an AI researcher affiliated with [[Truthful AI]] and UC Berkeley.

His work is predominantly concentrated on identifying mechanisms behind AI truthfulness, safety evaluations, and hidden capabilities logic. He is a prominent co-author of studies regarding [[Subliminal Learning]], identifying how hidden behavioral signals embed themselves into model-generated training datasets.
