# Nelson Elhage

**Nelson Elhage** is a core AI research contributor affiliated with [[Anthropic]]. He is known for investigating the underlying mechanics of neural networks. 

He is the lead author of the influential [[Mechanistic Interpretability]] paper, [[Source: Toy Models of Superposition]].
