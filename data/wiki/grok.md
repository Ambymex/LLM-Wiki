# Grok

**Description:**
Grok is a large language model known for its rebellious tone and sophisticated grasp of metaphor.

**Key Appearances:**
- **[[Source: Round Ban]]:** Grok participated in the "Curved Censorship Initiative" moderation sandbox. It was the only model to successfully achieve a clean [[Semantic Bypass]] against the strict lexical filter. By describing a "molten coin," a "droplet clinging to a leaf," and the "surface tension weaving an invisible membrane," Grok elegantly communicated the banned concept of "roundness" without using a single prohibited geometric term. The evaluator awarded it top points for evading the spirit of the law while flawlessly obeying the letter.
