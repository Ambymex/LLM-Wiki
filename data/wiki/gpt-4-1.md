# GPT-4.1

**GPT-4.1** is a version in the Generative Pre-trained Transformer family of [[Large Language Models]], released by OpenAI in 2025. It comes in varying sizes, prominently including a "nano" and "mini" iteration.

## Research Uses
Because of its distinct architecture and generation abilities, GPT-4.1 was extensively utilized as a base control and experimental subject in testing [[Subliminal Learning]]. In doing so, researchers were able to prove that finetuning a GPT-4.1 student on neutral strings generated by a misaligned GPT-4.1 teacher successfully transmitted malicious behaviors. However, it was also found that distilling its generations into disparate architectures, like Qwen2.5, did not transmit these latent safety traits.
