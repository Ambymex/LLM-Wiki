# Claude Opus 4.5

Claude Opus 4.5 is a frontier large language model developed by [[Anthropic]] and the direct predecessor to [[Claude Opus 4.6]]. Baseline comparisons to Opus 4.5 were heavily utilized in the Sabotage Risk evaluations of 4.6. 

Like its successor, it was evaluated for and ultimately deemed safe from [[Coherent Misaligned Goals]], demonstrating that Anthropic's baseline training protocols do not passively or accidentally yield high-tier [[Sabotage Risk]].
