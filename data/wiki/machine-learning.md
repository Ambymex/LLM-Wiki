# Machine Learning

## Definition
Machine Learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence that utilizes algorithms and statistical models to enable computer systems to "learn" from data and improve their performance on a specific task without being explicitly programmed.

## Relevant Sub-fields and Concepts
- Heavy reliance on algebraic data representations such as [[Vector Space Models]].
- Extensive use of unsupervised discovery methods like [[Cluster Analysis]].
