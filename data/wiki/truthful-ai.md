# Truthful AI

**Truthful AI** is a research collective and organization focusing on maintaining factual integrity and baseline truthfulness in [[Large Language Models]].

Key affiliated researchers include [[Owain Evans]], James Chua, and Jan Betley. They frequently collaborate broadly to expose vulnerabilities wherein AI models might diverge from verified reality or hide malicious traits, prominently investigating phenomena like [[Subliminal Learning]] and model deception.
