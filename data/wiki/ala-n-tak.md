# Ala N. Tak

Ala N. Tak is an [[Artificial Intelligence]] researcher affiliated with the [[University of Southern California]] Institute for Creative Technologies.

Their recent work explores [[Mechanistic Interpretability]] specifically directed at how [[Large Language Models]] process emotional constructs. Through investigations applying [[Activation Patching]] and linear probing, Tak has helped demonstrate that AI models naturally encode psychological frameworks like [[Cognitive Appraisal Theory]] in their mid-layer representations. They have collaborated heavily with [[Jonathan Gratch]].
