# GPT-5.6

**GPT-5.6** is a frontier model developed by [[OpenAI]]. 

As of June 2026, GPT-5.6 exists under strict [[Government AI Control]], released only to government-approved partners on a tightly restricted, customer-by-customer base. OpenAI CEO Sam Altman admitted internally that this gating was not the company's "preferred long-term model," but rather a requirement of regulatory compliance, mirroring the restricted handling of Anthropic's [[Mythos 5]].
