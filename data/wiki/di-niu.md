# Di Niu

**Di Niu** is an artificial intelligence researcher and faculty member at the [[University of Alberta]]. 

Alongside [[Amirhosein Ghasemabadi]], Di Niu co-authored foundational research on internal circuit monitoring, notably introducing the [[Gnosis Mechanism]] to establish intrinsic hallucination prediction inside [[Large Language Models]] independent of external text evaluators.
