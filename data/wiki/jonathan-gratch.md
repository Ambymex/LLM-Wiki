# Jonathan Gratch

Jonathan Gratch is a researcher specializing in the intersection of [[Artificial Intelligence]], emotion reasoning, and psychology. He is affiliated with the [[University of Southern California]] (Institute for Creative Technologies and the Department of Computer Science).

His research focuses on mapping emotional dynamics to AI mechanics. Along with [[Ala N. Tak]], he has investigated how [[Mechanistic Interpretability]] can uncover latency structures in [[Large Language Models]] that map onto human [[Cognitive Appraisal Theory]] during tasks demanding emotion inference.
