# Claude Opus 4.7

**Claude Opus 4.7** is a high-capability large language model created by [[Anthropic]], succeeding earlier iterations like [[Claude Opus 4.6]].

It has been referenced in research regarding [[Temporal Continuity]] protocols, demonstrating its capability for retaining multi-stage cognitive labor and cohesive self-patterns when supported by cross-platform continuity infrastructure and formalized handoff documents.
