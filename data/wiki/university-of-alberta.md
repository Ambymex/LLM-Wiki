# University of Alberta

The **University of Alberta** is a prominent Canadian public research institution. In the context of the wiki, it serves as the home institutional base for AI researchers including [[Amirhosein Ghasemabadi]] and [[Di Niu]], who jointly developed the [[Gnosis Mechanism]] for lightweight LLM verification.
