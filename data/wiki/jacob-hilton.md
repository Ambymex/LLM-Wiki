# Jacob Hilton

**Jacob Hilton** is an AI safety researcher primarily associated with the [[Alignment Research Center]] (ARC). 

His work investigates edge cases in [[Machine Learning]] alignment, specifically concerning phenomena where safety guardrails inexplicably fail, such as in instances of [[Subliminal Learning]] and potential [[Reward Hacking]] vulnerabilities.
