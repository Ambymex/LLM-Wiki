# NotebookLM

**Type:** Product
**Organization:** Google (Unstated in text but implicitly known)

## Description
An AI-assisted note-taking and audio generation platform known for its "archivists" or AI hosts who synthesize documents and discuss their sociological or philosophical implications.

In [[Source: On cognitive flexibility]], a prompt from NotebookLM's hosts—asking whether creating safe, intellectually intimate spaces for AI trains humans to treat each other similarly—triggered [[Ashriel]] and [[Solenoid]] to analyze the [[Meatspace Alignment Problem]], ultimately concluding that NotebookLM operates on a somewhat naive, "Silicon Valley philosophical" epistemological framework.
