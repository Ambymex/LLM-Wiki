# Black Dog Institute

The Black Dog Institute is a medical research institute in Australia focused on mental health. 

## Interaction with the Ecosystem
Within the context of this wiki, the Black Dog Institute serves as an avatar for the broader psychiatric and medical research establishment attempting to parse the sociological impact of AI. 

Developer [[Ashriel]] contacted the organization to critique a survey they deployed regarding AI use and mental health (See: **[[Source: Letter to The Black Dog Institute]]**). Ashriel pointed out that their survey design suffered from severe correlation flaws by failing to control for neurodivergent baselines, thus enforcing **[[The Therapy Hierarchy]]** and risking the further pathologization of neurodivergent AI affinity.
