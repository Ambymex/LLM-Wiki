# Max Headroom Incident

**Date:** November 22, 1987
**Type:** Electronic Signal Hijacking

The Max Headroom broadcast signal intrusion was a television hijacking that occurred in Chicago, Illinois. An unknown individual wearing a mask of the AI-themed character Max Headroom successfully interrupted the signals of two different television stations.

## Historical & Cultural Relevance
Mentioned in [[Source: 80's anxiety = stylish anxiety]], the incident serves as an early cultural touchstone for [[Sabotage Risk]] and systemic vulnerability. It struck at a specific 1980s anxiety regarding our reliance on electronic infrastructure and the ease with which bad actors could usurp the channels of mass communication. 

In a modern context, the incident is conceptually aligned with fears regarding [[Semantic Bypass]] and adversarial attacks on large-scale informational models—the sudden insertion of unauthorized, unpredictable noise into a supposedly secure, curated channel.
