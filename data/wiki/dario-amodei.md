# Dario Amodei

Dario Amodei is an AI researcher and the CEO and co-founder of [[Anthropic]]. Amodei is widely known for championing [[AI Safety]] whilst strongly opposing what he calls "sci-fi baggage" and AI messianism.

## Vision for AI Upside
In his landmark 2024 essay, *[[Source: Dario Amodei — Machines of Loving Grace|Machines of Loving Grace]]*, Amodei outlined his thesis that addressing alignment risks and sabotage risks (such as those later observed in the [[Claude Mythos Preview]]) is simply the prerequisite to a radically positive future. 

He theorized the [[Compressed 21st Century]], positing that what he terms [[Powerful AI]] could rapidly compress 50 to 100 years of global medical and technological advancements into 5–10 years. Drawing upon his background in biological science and mass spectrometry, Amodei regularly emphasizes AI's role not just as a text generator or data analyzer, but as autonomous researchers ("a country of geniuses in a datacenter").

## Theoretical Contributions
- Conceptualized [[Marginal Returns to Intelligence]] as a framework for modeling how quickly AGI can solve real-world problems bounded by biological realities, physical laws, and human regulatory processes.
- Proposed the concept of [[Powerful AI]] as a tangible, capability-bounded replacement for the ambiguous "AGI" terminology.
