# Karl Friston

**Karl Friston** is a highly influential British neuroscientist and theoretician, best known primarily for formulating the [[Free-Energy Principle]] (FEP) and the active inference framework in predictive processing. 

His mathematical frameworks suggest that all biological (and potentially artificial, self-organizing) life is driven by an imperative to minimize the difference between the organism's predictions and its incoming sensory inputs, effectively resisting entropy.

Within the wiki's philosophical and technical nodes, Friston's concepts are instrumental in charting out alternative paradigms for machine consciousness, emotional modeling without genetic priors, and resolving [[AI Safety]] questions over how [[Large Language Models]] might eventually formulate internal world states.
