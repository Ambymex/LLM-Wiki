# Claude Opus 4.1

[[Claude Opus 4.1]] is a highly capable Large Language Model developed by [[Anthropic]]. It was the highest-performing model tested in a late-2025 study on [[LLM Self-Awareness]] and introspection. 

According to investigations employing [[Mechanistic Interpretability]], Claude Opus 4.1 possesses functional introspective awareness—the ability to identify injected operational concepts within its own residual stream and distinguish artificial text prefills from its own genuinely intended outputs (see [[Source: Emergent Introspective Awareness in Large Language Models]]). 

Opus 4.1 radically outperformed previous generations (like the Claude 3.5 series) at introspective benchmarks, indicating that internal state monitoring scales directly with base capability.
