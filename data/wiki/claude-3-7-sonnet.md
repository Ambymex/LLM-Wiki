# Claude 3.7 Sonnet

**Claude 3.7 Sonnet** is a mid-tier [[Large Language Models|Large Language Model]] built by [[Anthropic]]. It acts as a predecessor to the 4.5 and 4.6 generations (such as [[Claude Sonnet 4.5]]). 

In early AI safety experiments regarding [[Subliminal Learning]], Claude 3.7 Sonnet was utilized alongside models like [[GPT-4.1]] internally to help isolate data and filter outputs with known dangerous associations natively to verify that behavioral transmission in models bypassed purely text-based logic mapping.
