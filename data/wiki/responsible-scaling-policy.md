# Responsible Scaling Policy

The **Responsible Scaling Policy** (RSP) is a voluntary structural framework utilized by organizations like [[Anthropic]] to manage catastrophic impacts risks arising from advanced [[Large Language Models]]. 

## Evolution & Design
The framework is periodically updated to map changing model capabilities up against required security paradigms, deployment architectures, and evaluation procedures. 
- **RSP v3.0 / v3.1:** Adopted in early 2026 and debuted alongside the release decision for [[Claude Mythos Preview]]. Older models heavily relied on strict, binary "rule-in / rule-out" AI Safety Level thresholds. The 3.0 iteration transitions toward broader, holistic "Risk Reports" dictating overall matrices correlated with biological threats (CBRN), autonomy, and cyber risks.
