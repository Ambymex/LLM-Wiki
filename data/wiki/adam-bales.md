# Adam Bales

Adam Bales is a researcher working at the intersection of AI ethics, political philosophy, and sociology (often associated with Google DeepMind).

He is a co-author of the 2026 paper *[[Source: Artificial Minds, Human Disagreement]]*, where he and [[Iason Gabriel]] analyzed the political and societal challenges that arise from public disagreement over [[Machine Consciousness]] and [[Machine Devotion]].
