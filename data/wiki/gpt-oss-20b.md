# OpenAI gpt-oss-20B

**gpt-oss-20B** is an open-source, Mixture of Experts (MoE) model released by [[OpenAI]] and documented around 2025 (Agarwal et al., 2025). 

It is notable in AI research as a public testbed architecture for investigating structural internal signals. For example, it served as a frozen test backbone alongside the [[Qwen]] parameters during the development of the [[Gnosis Mechanism]] for self-verifying [[Large Language Models]].
