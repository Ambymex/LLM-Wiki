# Amirhosein Ghasemabadi

**Amirhosein Ghasemabadi** is an artificial intelligence researcher affiliated with the [[University of Alberta]].

He is the co-author (along with [[Di Niu]]) of the 2025 paper detailing the [[Gnosis Mechanism]], which demonstrated that zero-shot, dynamic inspection of internal loops and circuits within [[Large Language Models]] can accurately predict hallucinations and computational failures.
