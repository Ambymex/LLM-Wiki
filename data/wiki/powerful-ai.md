# Powerful AI

Powerful AI is a descriptive substitute for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) meant to concretize what highly advanced [[Large Language Models]] (or their successors) actually look like when they reach super-human capacity.

The term was crystallized by [[Dario Amodei]] in his thesis [[Source: Dario Amodei — Machines of Loving Grace]] as a deliberate move away from the quasi-religious or "sci-fi baggage" associated with AGI definitions.

Amodei defines it as "a country of geniuses in a datacenter" exhibiting these precise capabilities:
- Intelligence exceeding a Nobel Prize winner across relevant fields (biology, programming, math, engineering, etc.).
- Complete operative access to digital interfaces (text, audio, video, keyboard control, coding environments).
- Deep autonomy capable of executing hours-, days-, or weeks-long tasks recursively.
- Speed 10x-100x faster than humans when absorbing information or generating actions.
- Capable of being replicated into millions of instances acting independently or collaborating in specialized subsets. 

Such a system acts as the underlying catalyst for the [[Compressed 21st Century]] by maximizing the [[Marginal Returns to Intelligence]].
